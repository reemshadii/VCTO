üé® Color Analysis

How does the color analysis work?
Our AI uses a sophisticated 3-step process: 1. Feature Extraction (Computer Vision):
  • Uses Google's MediaPipe to detect 478 facial landmarks
  • Samples color from 9 skin zones (forehead, cheeks, jaw)
  • Analyzes hair color from 3 regions (detects natural color even with highlights)
  • Extracts eye color from both irises using advanced filtering
2. Color Space Analysis (CIELAB):
  • Converts RGB to CIELAB (perceptually uniform color space)
  • Calculates temperature (warm vs cool undertones)
  • Measures contrast (difference between skin, hair, eyes)
  • Determines depth (light vs dark features)
3. Season Classification (12-Season System):
  • Categorizes you into one of 4 seasons (Spring, Summer, Autumn, Winter)
  • Refines to 12 sub-seasons (Bright/True/Light Spring, etc.)
  • Generates personalized color palette (5 signature colors)
  • Provides confidence score (High/Medium/Low based on clarity of features)
Result: You get a scientifically-backed color palette that harmonizes with your natural coloring!
What if I have dyed hair?
Simply check the "My hair is currently dyed" box in Step 1! When selected, our AI will:
  • üéØ Use your eyebrow color instead of hair color for analysis
  • üìä Focus more heavily on skin undertone for temperature determination
  • üëÅÔ∏è Give extra weight to eye color in the classification
Why eyebrows? Eyebrow hair is the closest genetic match to your natural hair color and usually remains undyed or fades slower than scalp hair. Note: If you've also dyed your eyebrows, the analysis may be less accurate. Consider waiting until roots grow out, or use a photo from before dyeing if available.
